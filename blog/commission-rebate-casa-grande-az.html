How Rebates Work in Casa Grande

In many Casa Grande transactions, the seller or builder offers compensation to the buyer’s agent. When structured properly, a portion of that compensation may be returned to the buyer as a credit reflected on the settlement statement.

Financing vs. Cash

If financing, lender guidelines determine how credits are applied. For cash purchases, rebate structures are generally more flexible but still must be documented through escrow.
