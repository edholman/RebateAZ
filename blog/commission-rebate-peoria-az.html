How Rebates Work in Peoria

In many Peoria transactions, the seller offers compensation to the buyer’s agent. When structured properly, part of that compensation may be returned to the buyer as a closing credit on the settlement statement.

New Construction Considerations

Peoria has many new build communities. If you’re considering new construction, make sure your agent is registered with the builder on the first visit. Builder policies vary, and registration timing can affect eligibility for buyer agent compensation.
