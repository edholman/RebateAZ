How Rebates Work in Avondale

In many Avondale transactions, the seller offers compensation to the buyer’s agent. When structured properly, part of that compensation may be returned to the buyer as a credit at closing. This credit typically appears on the settlement statement.

Financing Notes

If financing, lender guidelines determine how credits are applied and whether any limits apply. For cash purchases, the structure may be more flexible but should still be documented through escrow.
